**Writing Guidelines:**

- Be specific and measurable in all requirements
- Use clear, jargon-free language that stakeholders can understand
- Include concrete examples and user scenarios
- Prioritize features as P0 (critical), P1 (important), P2 (nice-to-have)
- Make assumptions explicit and call out dependencies
- Focus on the "what" and "why" rather than the "how"
